Snorkeling and scuba diving in Tobago with CocoMotion

Snorkeling and scuba diving are two of the most exciting ways to experience Tobago's natural beauty. Add to that the excitement of a high speed ride aboard Coco Motion, a 31ft cigarette hulled offshore powerboat. The powerful twin engines can whisk you to some of the best snorkeling spots along Tobago's lush and lovely coastline. If scuba diving is more to your liking, we can show you some of the most exciting seascapes and marine life in, not only Tobago, but the Caribbean.

Snorkeling

Our 3 hour snorkeling tours, which leave at 9:00am or 1:00pm, whiz you along Tobago's Caribbean coast, past secluded tree-lined beaches and bays accessible only from the sea. On the way, keep an eye out for dolphins, which love the speed and like to play around the boat. We moor at secluded bays and locations that offer excellent snorkeling opportunities and breathtaking scenery, both underwater and above. There is plenty of time for enjoying the water or simply relaxing on board. Life jackets, for those who prefer to use them, and full snorkeling equipment are provided. We cater for all abilities and experience levels, from the new snorkeler to those with many snorkeling adventures behind them already. The boat has a bimini top which we put up while we are moored to provide a shady spot for those who don't want too much sun, however we always recommend bringing a good waterproof sunblock to protect you from our bright Tobago sunshine. During the tour we serve soft drinks, bottled water and our famous home-made Carrot Cake! We finish the tour around noon (or 4pm for afternoon tours). What a wonderful way to spend a morning or afternoon in Tobago.

Scuba

There's nothing else like it - especially when the experience is one-on-one with Marlon, a PADI certified Scuba Instructor with twenty years of diving experience in Tobago. The warm clear water surrounding Tobago offers the perfect opportunity to explore the underwater realm. Both experienced divers, and those new to the sport, can enjoy all the excitement and beauty that diving in Tobago has to offer. We specialize in small groups and families so you are assured of personalized attention. If you would like to try Scuba for the first time, the Discover Scuba Diving program is perfect for the newcomer who would like a "try dive" before enrolling in a certification course. If you would like to earn your entry level certification or perhaps continue your diving education, we offer all the PADI courses from Scuba Diver to Divemaster. We are happy to schedule classes to suit your group or family. Not quite ready to try scuba? Our snorkeling trips offer the opportunity to view some of the amazing creatures that inhabit our coral reefs.

Charters

For the ultimate experience, CocoMotion is available for private charter for those who would like an exclusive trip on our luxury 31 ft power boat. What better way to celebrate a special birthday, wedding anniversary, honeymoon or just simply being on holiday? We are happy to tailor-make a tour to your individual requirements; snorkeling equipment, carrot cake, bottled water and soft drinks are provided. For full day charters we moor up for a delicious beach barbeque lunch, accompanied by local beers and home-made rum punch. Just let us know if you would like anything else on board for your special trip. It's like having your own private boat in Tobago!

About Us

Marlon and Rachel have been operating Cocomotion for over 15 years, and Marlon knows the waters around Tobago 'like the back of his hand', having been in the scuba diving business for even longer. Marlon is your captain and guide on all trips, while Rachel is responsible for the famous Cocomotion Carrot Cake that you will get to sample on board. Both Marlon and Rachel are PADI scuba instructors and are passionate about the marine life in our island's waters - our aim is show YOU the very best that Tobago has to offer, above and below the water!
